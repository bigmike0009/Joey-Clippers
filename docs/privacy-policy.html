Joe's Clippers Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 29, 2026

Joe's Clippers ("we," "our," or "us") is a private, invite-only booking app for Joe's barbershop members. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the app:

  • Account Information: email address and authentication data managed by our auth provider.
  • Profile Information: full name and role (admin or member).
  • Booking and Scheduling Data: bookings, cancellations, day requests, and invite status metadata.
  • Device and Technical Data: technical logs required to operate and secure the service.

2. How We Use Information

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Provide booking, cancellation, and scheduling functionality.
  • Allow admin management of shop days, requests, and member access.
  • Maintain service security and reliability.
  • Provide support when requested.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share information only with providers needed to run the app, including Supabase (authentication/database) and Expo/EAS (app delivery/operations).

4. Data Retention

We retain account and scheduling data while your account is active or as needed for legitimate business and legal purposes. When accounts are deleted, we delete or de-identify personal data unless retention is required by law.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • Access and update your profile information in-app.
  • Delete your account in-app from Profile > Delete Account.
  • Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion assistance.

6. Children

Joe's Clippers is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Security

We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. International Transfers

Information may be processed in countries other than your own where our service providers operate.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will revise the effective date and publish the updated policy.
