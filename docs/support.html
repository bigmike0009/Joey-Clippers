When You Contact Us

Please include:

  1. The email address on your account.
  2. A short description of the issue.
  3. What you were trying to do.
  4. Any error message shown in the app.
  5. Optional screenshots.

Common Issues

  1. I cannot register: Registration is invite-only. You must use a valid invite link from Joe.
  2. Invite expired or used: Ask Joe to generate a new invite.
  3. I cannot book a day: The day may be full. Join the waitlist if shown.
  4. My account was revoked: Member access is managed by the admin. Contact Joe directly.
  5. I want to delete my account: In the app, go to Profile > Delete Account.

App Scope

Joe's Clippers is a private, iOS-first app for a closed member group. The app does not offer in-app purchases or subscriptions.
