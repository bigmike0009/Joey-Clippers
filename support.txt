Common Issues

1) I cannot register
Joe's Clippers is invite-only. You must use a valid invite link from Joe.
If your invite says expired or already used, ask Joe to generate a new invite.

2) I forgot my password
Use the password reset flow provided by the authentication screen.
If you still cannot access your account, contact support.

3) I cannot book a day
If a day is full, booking is unavailable. You can join the waitlist when shown.
If booking fails unexpectedly, try again and refresh.

4) My account was revoked
Member access is managed by the admin. Contact Joe directly if you believe this was a mistake.

5) I want to delete my account
In the app, go to Profile > Delete Account.
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
