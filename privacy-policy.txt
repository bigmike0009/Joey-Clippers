Joe's Clippers Privacy Policy
Effective Date: April 29, 2026

Joe's Clippers ("we," "our," or "us") is a private, invite-only booking app for Joe's barbershop members.
This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the app:

- Account Information
	- Email address
	- Encrypted authentication credentials (managed by our authentication provider)

- Profile Information
	- Full name
	- Role (admin or member)

- Booking and Scheduling Data
	- Shop day bookings and cancellations
	- Day requests you submit
	- Invite status and related metadata

- Device and Technical Data
	- Basic technical logs needed to operate and secure the service
	- We do not use your data for advertising tracking

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Let you book, cancel, and view appointments
- Let the admin manage shop days, requests, and member access
- Maintain app security and prevent misuse
- Provide support when you contact us

3. Legal Basis (Where Applicable)

We process data as needed to provide the service you request, for legitimate interests in running a secure scheduling service, and to comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.
We share data only with service providers required to run the app, such as:

- Supabase (authentication and database infrastructure)
- Expo/EAS services (app delivery and operations)

These providers process data under their own terms and security controls.

5. Data Retention

We keep account and scheduling data for as long as your account is active, or as needed for legitimate business and legal purposes.
If you delete your account, we delete or de-identify personal data unless retention is required by law.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You can:

- Access and update your profile information in the app
- Delete your account in the app from Profile > Delete Account
- Contact us to request data access or deletion help

Depending on your region, you may have additional privacy rights.

7. Children

Joe's Clippers is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect personal information.
No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we continuously work to protect your data.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, where our service providers operate.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and post the revised policy.
